# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive community focused on building reliable software.

Participation in this project is open to everyone who engages constructively and treats others with dignity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in language and tone.
- Focus discussions on technical work and project outcomes.
- Assume good intent and provide actionable feedback.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them quickly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Hate speech, slurs, or demeaning language toward any group.
- Discrimination based on personal or protected characteristics.
- Sexually explicit, violent, or abusive language directed at others.
- Trolling, baiting, or repeated disruptive behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove or edit content, issue warnings, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban contributors for behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers through the repository's private contact method. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by common open-source community standards, including the Contributor Covenant.
